About Us

Inspiration

We have been inspired to create our own music by many things, here are some of them.


Spencer has mainly been inspired by all the great vocalists in the metal community including Will Ramos of Lorna Shore, Ryan Vail of Larcenia Roe, Nicolas TFB of Ruins of Perception, etc... mainly for the unique sounds and noises these vocalists can make is what has inspired me to become a vocalist myself.

Lorenzo has mainly been inspired by all the great guitarists in the metal community including Manuel Gardner of Unprocessed, Tim Henson of Polyphia, Steve Vai, etc... mainly for their unique sounds and techniques that they use to create the music they do.

Parker has mainly been inspired by all the great bands and their guitarists in the metal community including Synyster Gates of Avenged Sevenfold, Dimebag Darrell of Pantera, John Petrucci of Dream Theater, etc... mainly for their styles and techniques that they use to create the music they do.

Our Music

We are aiming to make music that will immerse our listeners in our music and will come back wanting even more by mixing different sub genres of metal together to create the unique sound of Horizons of Avarice

We currently have a bandcamp page with lyric releases for our music we are hoping to create in the future as we are currently working on making releasing our first songs.



Members
