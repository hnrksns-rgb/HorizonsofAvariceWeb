Inspiration

We have been inspired to create our own music by many things, here are some of them.
"Spencer has mainly been inspired by all the great vocalists in the metal community including Will Ramos of Lorna Shore, Ryan Vail of Larcenia Roe, Nicolas TFB, etc... mainly for the unique sounds and noises these vocalists can make is what has inspired me to become a vocalist myself."
"Lorenzo has mainly been inspired by all the great guitarists in the metal community including Manuel Gardner of Oceano, Tim Henson of Polyphia, Steve Vai, etc... mainly for their unique sounds and techniques that they use to create the music they do."
"Parker has mainly been inspired by all the great guitarists in the metal commubity including Syn Gates of Periphery, Dimebag Darrell of Pantera, John Pertrucci of Dream Theater, etc... mainly for their styles and techniques that they use to create the music they do."

Our Music

We are aiming to make music that will immerse our listeners in our music and will come back wanting even more by mixing different sub genres of metal together to create the unique sound of Horizons of Avarice
